LIFE ON LAND

PROBLEMS

Rapid Decline

In the Philippines, land degradation is driven by deforestation, illegal logging, and unregulated urbanization. From 2015 to 2019, the country's forests and productive lands were significantly impacted, contributing to reduced agricultural productivity, increased disaster risks, and loss of biodiversity.


Conservation Standstill

Despite efforts to protect biodiversity, the expansion of protected areas in the Philippines has slowed since 2015. This stagnation threatens the conservation of endemic species and critical habitats, undermining national and global biodiversity goals.


Mass Extinction Crisis

The Philippines is at the forefront of this crisis, with many species facing extinction due to habitat loss, climate change, and illegal wildlife trade. The country's rich biodiversity is under severe threat, and urgent conservation actions are needed to prevent further losses.



PHILIPPINE GOALS

Urgent Measures Against Poaching and Wildlife Trafficking

Sustainably manage and protect marine and Take urgent action to end poaching and trafficking of protected species of flora and fauna and address both demand and supply of illegal wildlife products

Conservation of Ecosystems and Freshwater Resources

By 2020, ensure the conservation, restoration and sustainable use of terrestrial and inland freshwater ecosystems and their services, in particular forests, wetlands, mountains and drylands, in line with obligations under international agreements.

Managing Invasive Species in Ecosystems

By 2020, introduce measures to prevent the introduction and significantly reduce the impact of invasive alien species on land and water ecosystems and control or eradicate the priority species.

GOVERNMENT PROJECTS

Protected Area Development and Management

This initiative focuses on conserving and managing the Philippines' protected areas, which are critical for biodiversity and ecosystem services. It involves enhancing the legal framework, improving infrastructure, and engaging local communities in conservation efforts.

Protection and Conservation of Wildlife

This program aims to safeguard the country's wildlife through habitat protection, anti-poaching efforts, and the rehabilitation of endangered species, ensuring the survival of the Philippines' unique biodiversity.

Enhanced National Greening Program

This program is a large-scale reforestation effort aimed at restoring degraded forests, enhancing carbon sequestration, and improving the livelihoods of communities through sustainable forestry practices.
